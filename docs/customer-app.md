# Customer App

The customer app is the booking experience for people visiting a barbershop. Customers use it to book appointments, track loyalty rewards, and manage their profile — all from their phone.

---

## Getting Started

Customers sign in using their phone number. A one-time code is sent via SMS to verify identity. Social login (Google, Apple) is also supported. No username or password is required.

On first launch, the app loads the shop's branding and available services automatically.

---

## Home

The home screen gives customers a snapshot of what's relevant right now:

- A welcome message personalized to the customer
- Whether the shop is available today and from what time
- A quick rebook option for their most recent service
- Active campaigns or special offers from the shop
- A summary of their loyalty points and progress toward the next reward

---

## Booking an Appointment

Booking follows a guided step-by-step flow:

1. **Choose services** — browse or search the service menu, with categories (Haircuts, Shaves, Treatments, Add-ons). Multiple services can be selected in one booking.
2. **Choose a staff member** — pick a specific barber or choose "Any Available" to see the earliest open slot.
3. **Pick a date and time** — a calendar shows available days, and open time slots are displayed for the chosen date.
4. **Review and confirm** — a summary shows the selected services, total duration, price, assigned staff, and cancellation policy. An optional notes field lets customers add a personal message to the barber.

Once confirmed, the customer receives a booking reference and can view the appointment immediately.

---

## Managing Appointments

The appointments tab shows all upcoming and past bookings.

**Upcoming appointments** show the date, time, staff, and services. Customers can:
- Cancel an appointment (subject to the shop's cancellation policy)
- View full appointment details

**Past appointments** show completed bookings with their status. Customers can:
- Rebook the same service with the same barber in one tap
- Leave a review for the appointment

---

## Leaving a Review

After a completed appointment, customers can rate their experience from 1 to 5 stars and leave an optional written comment. Reviews can be submitted anonymously. Customers can also update or delete a review they've already left.

---

## Loyalty & Rewards

The loyalty section shows the customer's current standing in the shop's rewards program:

- **Points balance** — current points available to spend
- **Points history** — a full ledger of points earned and redeemed
- **Rewards catalog** — available rewards the customer can redeem, showing the cost in points
- **Locked rewards** — rewards not yet reachable, with the points still needed displayed

To redeem a reward, the customer selects it in the app and presents the confirmation to the barber at the shop.

---

## Profile & Settings

Customers can update their personal information (name, phone number, profile photo) and manage their preferences:

- **Language** — switch between Turkish and English
- **Notifications** — control which alerts are received
- **Favorite staff** — designate a preferred barber

Sessions can be reviewed and any active session can be revoked remotely. Customers can log out from the profile screen.
