# Berberim — Product Overview

## What is Berberim?

Berberim is an appointment booking and business management platform built for barbershops and salons. It connects customers with their preferred barbers through a simple mobile booking experience, while giving shop owners and staff a full set of tools to run their day-to-day operations.

The platform serves three distinct groups of users:

- **Customers** — people who want to discover, book, and manage appointments at a barbershop
- **Tenant Staff & Admins** — barbers and shop owners who manage appointments, customers, and shop settings
- **Platform Admins** — Berberim operators who manage the businesses running on the platform

---

## Core Concepts

### Tenant
A *tenant* represents a single barbershop or salon on the platform. Each tenant has its own staff, services, schedule, and customer base. Tenants are fully isolated from one another.

### Appointment
An appointment is the central unit of activity. A customer books a time slot with a specific staff member for one or more services. Appointments move through a lifecycle: confirmed → payment received → completed (or cancelled / no-show).

### Loyalty Program
Each tenant can run a points-based loyalty program. Customers earn points on completed appointments and redeem them for rewards defined by the shop. The program is fully configurable per tenant.

### Staff & Services
Staff members are assigned to specific services they offer. Each service has a name, duration, and price. Customers browse staff and services when booking.

---

## The Two Apps

### Customer App
The customer-facing mobile app used to browse, book, and manage appointments. Customers also track their loyalty points and leave reviews.

### Tenant App
The staff and admin mobile app used to manage the shop. Admins control settings, staff, services, and analytics. Staff members manage their schedule and handle appointments day-to-day.
